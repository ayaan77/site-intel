# Site Intel

> **AI-Powered Website Intelligence & Competitor Analysis**

## Problem Statement
Businesses struggle to manually analyze competitor websites, understand their tech stacks, and identify conversion optimization opportunities. Existing tools are fragmented, expensive, or require deep technical expertise.

## Solution
Site Intel is an AI-native platform that provides instant, deep intelligence on any website. It combines:
1.  **Tech Stack Detection**: Identifies frameworks, analytics, and tools.
2.  **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Audits**: AI-driven analysis of landing pages with scored reports.
3.  **Competitive Intelligence**: "Spy Mode" to uncover hidden strategies.
4.  **Generative UI Specs**: "Architect Mode" to turn insights into actionable development specifications.

## Target Audience
-   **Founders & Indie Hackers**: Rapidly analyzing competitors.
-   **Product Managers**: Benchmarking features and UX.
-   **Developers**: Understanding how sites are built.
-   **Marketers**: stealing (ethically) high-converting copy strategies.

## Core Capabilities
-   **Chat Interface**: Conversational interaction with "Gabriel" (the AI Architect).
-   **Multi-Mode AI**: Specialized personas (Architect, Roaster, CTO, Spy).
-   **Live Scraping**: Real-time fetching of website content and GitHub repositories.
-   **Visual Reports**: CRO scorecards and tech stack visualizations.
